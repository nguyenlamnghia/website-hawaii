Who We Are

About Wellbeing Navigators Hawaii

Wellbeing Navigators Hawaii is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization headquartered in Waipahu, Hawaiʻi. Founded in 2024, we are dedicated to navigating individuals, families, and communities through the complexities of mental and emotional wellness.

Our team believes that wellbeing is a journey, not a destination. We provide culturally sensitive guidance, resources, and support programs that honor the unique spirit of the Hawaiian community while addressing the growing need for accessible mental health services throughout Oʻahu and beyond.

Led by representative Kalei Kaanoi, our organization brings together trained wellness advocates, community leaders, and mental health allies committed to creating a healthier, more resilient Hawaiʻi.

Our Wellness Programs

Comprehensive, community-driven programs designed to support mental health and wellbeing for all ages across Hawaiʻi.

🧠

Mental Health Navigation

Connecting individuals and families with the right mental health resources, counselors, and support networks tailored to their unique needs across the Hawaiian Islands.

Community Wellness Workshops

Interactive workshops focused on stress management, emotional resilience, healthy relationships, and self-care practices for individuals of all backgrounds.

🌱

Youth Wellbeing Initiative

Empowering young people in Waipahu and across Oʻahu with tools for emotional intelligence, social connection, and mental health awareness.

🌊

Holistic Wellness Retreats

Nature-based and culturally grounded retreats that restore balance and foster a deeper connection to oneself and the land of Hawaiʻi.

📞

Crisis Resource Navigation

Guiding individuals in crisis toward immediate, appropriate professional support — ensuring no one faces their hardest moments alone in our community.

What is Wellbeing Navigators Hawaii? +

Yes. Wellbeing Navigators Hawaii is a federally recognized 501(c)(3) nonprofit. All donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law under Internal Revenue Code Section 501(c)(3). Our EIN is 84-4638431.
